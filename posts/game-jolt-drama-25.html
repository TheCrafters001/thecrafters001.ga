GameJolt Drama

Posted on 09/28/2020 8:54 PM by TheCrafters001

So. GameJolt has been having a lot of "drama" recently. I won't get into the details, and I am not going to say what users are involved, but I do think it needs to stop. All of this is just crazy, and I want it to stop. GameJolt is just a place for people to have fun, play games, and develop them, all with a loving community. I hate that the GameJolt Moderators are having to deal with all of this in the time of a pandemic, not only that, but things are just getting out of hand. One user on the site put it as

I really, REALLY don't like having to use my platform this way, to call specific people out, but enough is enough. I've had it with the toxicity in this community and I am VERY disappointed! - Anonymous (For user's privacy)

I agree with this. I even made a comment on the post, and he agree with most of what I had to say. I just don't want to see this kind of crap anymore. Pardon my language, but it's true. I have seen users leave the site over this ordeal, and I hate to see it. Many accounts are going in-active just because of everything going on. I don't want to join that group. I like the site, and I hate that this is happening. So please, with all the kindness from my heart, please stop fighting, or causing controversy. It annoys users like me, any many others.
