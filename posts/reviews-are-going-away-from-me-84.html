Reviews are going away from me.

Posted on 10/29/2020 7:01 AM by TheCrafters001

I know that I just added the reviews page for my site, but I am getting rid of it completely, as I do not want to review anything anymore. Know why? Scoring stuff is stupid. I won't miss reviews, but I am not going to do them anymore because scoring stuff can really make an impact on how someone sees something, and, if I say something that "hurts" someone, they will then start making comments on how that "Oh, YoU gAvE bLaNk A bAd ScOrE bEcAuSe YoU aRe NoT gOoD aT tHe GaMe" or "Oh, YoU gAvE bLaNk A bAd ScOrE bEcAuSe YoU aRe NoT gOoD aT uSiNg ThE sOfTwArE" and you know what? No body should feel attacked based on PERSONAL OPINION! Heck, even someone I watch said they are ditching their scoring system entirely. That video will be put at the end for you to watch, but I think he makes a ton of great points. Anyways, I have gotten rid of the reviews on my site already, if you can't tell. I just don't care for them anymore. Back when I reviewed games a long time ago (at least back in 2018), I only ever made 4 reviews before I shutdown that site. Why? Because I got old, real fast. I know there are people who like reviewing games, heck it is even their job (IGN), and I think that is a good job to have, but a lot of the time, most people just look at the score, and just say "This game looks good" or "7.8/10?! This game must be awful." And that is exactly what happens. I don't look at the score, or even the review. And, even if I do look at the score, I say "Okay, so this person gave it a 7/10. So they like it, but they think it could be just a little better." I never go on and email them claiming that their opinion is wrong. And a lot of the time, people also expect for them to update their reviews when a DLC drops. I have never seen that happen. Just because Pok√©mon Sword was very lackluster, doesn't mean the DLCs are going to make it better. Now, I am going to leave you off with this: If you are going to buy something, and look at the review, and that review is terrible, buy the game anyways, you may be surprised at what YOU like. That is all for now. Thanks for reading. - TheCrafters001 Here is that video I talked about before before.
