A long history.

A Long One Indeed.

Throughout 2019, I worked on and off the project, and I changed "Demos" to "Betas". But that does not change much. For a while, Betas did not come out as often as I wanted them to, but I was added stuff as fast as I can, BUT I always wanted quality over quantity. On Dec 4th, 2019, I pushed Pokémon Delta Green to GitHub. I made the project open-source, allowing anyone to add their own stuff to the game. And here we are today. There is not much more history to the game. The game has been being worked on ever since. I know it is a long history, but it is a intresting one. The span of 3 years.
