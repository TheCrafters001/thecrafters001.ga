Flash Player 2

Software Series: Solo Program

Available for: Windows 7 SP1 or Higher

Distributed Via: GameJolt, Itch.IO, GitHub

Want a free, yet powerful flash player, AND video player?

This is the right software. This software is a bundle between a flash player and and video player. Keep in mind that Adobe Flash Player (not included) is still required. The video player will start working automatically. This flash player allows you to play all your favorite games! Including:

  • Earn to Die
  • Earn to Die 2
  • Road of the Dead
  • Mutilate-a-Doll 2
  • Anything that has a flash version of it.

And the video player can play:

  • MP4
  • MP3
  • And a ton more!
  • Files

It also now includes an auto-updater. Stay up-to-date with the most current version!
