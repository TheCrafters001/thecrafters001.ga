Last post for 2020. (A post will be made in 2021.)

This year has been hard for many people. There is no denying that. Not everyone got COVID-19, but my heart goes out to those who were. And I would like to say, thank you, everyone. For everything that everyone has done to help make the Internet a better place throughout this whole Pandemic.

The TF2 Community is still in shock over what happened to Rick May. For those who don't know, Rick May was the voice of Soldier in a game called Team Fortress 2. He passed away due to COVID-19 on April 13th, 2020.

This year has been hard. Here is to a better year next year.
