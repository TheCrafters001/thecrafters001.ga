About Me

So... You may be wondering... who am I? Well... I am TheCrafters001. I am a hobbyist Software developer and Indie Game Dev. Originally I was known for my fan game, Pokemon Delta Green*, but now I am known as the guy who runs the TF2 Community on Game Jolt.

How I started

The way how I started... kinda an interesting story... Originally I wanted to make my own Steam Chat Bot. I found a tutorial online, and that is how I found out about Visual Studio. I would later find a Software called RPG Maker XP. This is where it all really started...

Green Applications

So... Originally I was calling myself Green Applications, because green is my favorite color. I later found out that there is a company called Green Applications, so I ended up dropping the name. But I kept the email I made for it, which is the same Email I use today for responding to questions. After hunting for a usable name, I settled with this one.

Dawn of The Arcane Town of Tidon

So... Originally I was making a game called "The Arcane Town of Tidon" in RPG Maker XP, but I never really finished it. I wanted to, but I never did. But at around the time of The Arcane Town of Tidon never happening, I wanted to make my own Pokemon Fan Game, as I am really into Pokemon.

Pokemon Essentials

It was around this time I found a tool called Pokemon Essentials. Today, it is a lot harder to get your hands on a copy of it, unless you know what you are doing... 

Anyways... Not much really happened. I still am working on that fan game, 5 years later.

Game Jolt Communities Added

So, some time in 2018, Game Jolt added Communities, and later added support for Custom Communities, I made a community based around the game Team Fortress 2, and it EXPLODED. And I mean it. I got 500 Members in 6-ish months. On top of that, it got Verified, and is currently sitting over 1,000 members (at the time of writing this).

Today

So we are at today. Not much more than that.
