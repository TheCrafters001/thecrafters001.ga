Charactors


Professor Midori is the Pokémon Professor of the Shorebarrow Region. He takes great pride in his work, and cares about people and Pokémon alike!


Gonzalos is your rival, and a friend! He meets you right after you get your first Pokémon, and challanges you to a battle! Unlike most trainers, he likes to battle for fun, and not to test their strength.
