Keep Control - Remastered

Keep Control - Remastered

Overview

Keep Control is a game I made originally for the GMTK 2020 GameJam. In that game you solve one puzzle, and then moved on to the boss. It was a quicky, but I really like how it came out, especially for a Jam game. You, the player, have to find out how to get out of the game that you are trapped in. The way to do that is to get to the end of the game (duh) and to meet someone… special, shall I say. That is all I can say right now, because all I have is like 4 lines of dialog, and that is it.
