When Is The Next Pokemon Delta Green Update?

11-13-2019
TheCrafters001

This post is regarding the next update in Pokemon Delta Green.
If you think this post is about how it is not going to be finished, your are very wrong. I have been working on this project for OVER 3 years now. I am not going to drop it just like that.

I want to say that the update may not be done for a while, as I am going to be working on a lot of bug fixes, story elements, and grammar/typo fixes. That means I am going to go through map by map and fix any issues with the dialog. I am also going to be going through and redesigning maps. This will have some-what of an effect on the story, because I may alter it to fit the town you are in.
That means I am also going to be working on the Gym Leaders. I will be working on their personalities this update. This way you can feel more attached to them. But before I start any of this, I am going to take a break. I will resume some time in December, after the GameOff Game Jam 2019 voting ends.
