Community Rules

User Rules

These rules apply to everyone in the community.

No Adult Content (words, images, videos, etc.)

Bannable offence

Ban Length: Forever (Posts will be reported & ejected**)

No Harassing Others

Bannable offence

First offence: 1 week (Posts will be ejected*)

Second offence: Forever. (Posts will be reported & ejected**)

Do NOT wish harm against others

Bannable offence

Ban Length: Forever (Posts will be reported & ejected**)

NO SPAMMING

Bannable offence

First offence: 1 week (Posts will be ejected*)

Second offence: 1 month (Posts will be ejected*)

Third offence: Forever (Posts will be ejected*)

No Impersonation

Bannable offence

Ban Length: Forever

Discussing other games are okay, but only in the channel “Random”

Non-bannable offence

Post will be moved to appropriate channel.

Music from the game can be posted, as long as it is in channel “Random”

Non-bannable offence

Post will be moved to appropriate channel

If you want to re-enact any TF2 Promotional video, please do it in channel “Memes” or channel “Heavy Is Dead”

Non-bannable offence

Post will be moved to appropriate channel

PLEASE don’t share personal info in ANY channel.

Bannable offence

Ban Length: 1 hour (Posts will be ejected*)

Don’t try Ban Evasion

Bannable offence

Ban Length: Forever (Posts will be ejected*)

Moderator Rules

These rules are made for the moderators of the TF2 Community on GameJolt. Normal members don't have to follow these rules.

Featuring Posts

Moderators are only allowed to feature 5 post per day, per moderator.

Reason: I feature way too many posts per day.

Note: These rules also go along with the GameJolt Site Guidelines. Please follow those rules as well.

* = every post you have made on the community will be ejected. Please do not repost them.

** = Only post that caused issue will be reported, and every post you have made on the community will be ejected. If you are un-banned, please do not repost them.

Rules were updated on: 11/24/2020. Please review them carefully.
